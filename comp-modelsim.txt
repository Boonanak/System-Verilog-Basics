- Open ModelSim from the terminal by entering "vsim"
- Navigate to the correct directory with cd, (make sure to switch the direction of the /'s with conpath
- Create a work library with vlib work
- Compile the design file(s) and tb(s) in one line with vlog "file.sv" "anotherfile.sv"
- In libary, navigate to the work folder you made and select the tb
- Drag important signals over to the wave viewer
- On the top select Simulate and then run-all
- Remember to select Zoom Full
